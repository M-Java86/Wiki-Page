Running

What made me become a runner

When I was a kid I would literally always run. My family would always tell me "Michelle!! please stop running before you hurt yourself, just walk". When I turned 5 years old is my mom put me in track and field. I went on the run AAU track and field every summer. Once I got into highschool, I began placing at state levels and even got full ride scholarships for college. Fast-forward to today I still love running on a competitive level. I have 3 full marathons under my belt and I'm training for my 4th one. Running is my peace.

Atlanta Track Club Runners World

Teaching

Why I started Teaching

My interest in teaching began college. During the summer I would volunteer abroad to teach young learners english. After college I went back Aboard for three months to teach adults english. Teaching english to those who don't speak it became very inspiring for me. I was giving others a chance to make their way to America to get better education and jobs.

Designing Sneakers

I love Sneakers

My first love for sneakers was 7. My parents purchased me a pair Jordan 1 high top bred. Every since I have been become a sneaker collector. In 2019 I became tired with the way sneakers had been made. I felt like it was something missing. I sketched out a design shopped them around. My first design got picked up by a company names Alive shoes, located in Italy. By september 2019 my shoes were out of production and ready to sell. I sold out the first week and there after. I recently got them back in production and as of October 30th they are available for purchase.
